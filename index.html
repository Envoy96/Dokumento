AFFIDAVIT



An affidavit is a written statement of facts voluntarily made by an affiant or deponent under an oath or affirmation administered by a person authorized to do so by law. It's used as evidence in court proceedings and various legal contexts. It carries legal weight and is often sworn before a notary public or another authorized officer to ensure its authenticity and truthfulness.
